# NakshatraAlpha

## Vision

NakshatraAlpha is a Financial Astrology Research Platform.

The objective is NOT to prove astrology.

The objective is to discover statistically significant relationships between astronomical parameters and financial market behaviour using scientific observation, hypothesis testing and historical validation.

Every rule must emerge from data.

No traditional astrological rule is accepted unless validated.

---

## Mission

Build the world's first research platform capable of objectively validating financial astrology.

## Research Principles

1. Observation Database is immutable.
2. Historical journals are never modified.
3. Concepts evolve.
4. Hypotheses can fail.
5. Failed hypotheses are valuable.
6. No confirmation bias.
7. Binary validation.
8. Data before belief.
9. Every rule requires statistical evidence.
10. Every prediction must be explainable.
