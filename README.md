# 🌌 NakshatraAlpha

Financial Astrology Research Platform combining Vedic astrology,
astronomical calculations and stock market research.

---

## Vision

NakshatraAlpha aims to build an institutional-grade financial astrology
research engine capable of analysing:

- Planetary positions
- Panchang calculations
- Nakshatra movements
- Market cycles
- Historical correlations
- Trading research
